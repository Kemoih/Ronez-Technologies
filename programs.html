Ronez Technologies Academic Programs

Elementary School

Foundation years focusing on reading, writing, and math skills, complemented by music, arts, and physical education.

Middle School

Building critical thinking and problem-solving skills in subjects like science, social studies, and
languages. Emphasis on teamwork, leadership, and personal growth.

High School

Preparing students for college and career readiness with advanced courses in subjects such as biology,
chemistry, physics, computer science, history, and foreign languages.

Extracurricular Activities

Sports teams, drama clubs, music ensembles, debate teams, and leadership programs to foster personal development.
